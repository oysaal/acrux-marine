Unforgetable Cruise Exxperience

SEYCHELLES! Untouched,unspoiled and unforgettable

Comprising of an archipelago of 115 islands in the Indian Ocean, the Seychelles is renowned for its fabulous serene beaches, breathtaking coral reefs and nature reserves. The East African island country boasts sustainable tourism that ensures that the untouched and unspoiled islands remain so. On a luxury cruise tour you can experience authentic island life at its very best. Explore the outstanding onshore and offshore attractions and marvel at the country’s spectacular and protected wildlife.


At any time of the year, a cruise around the stunning Seychelles archipelago allows you to discover both the amazing marine life and the majestic mountain rainforests that provide the perfect backdrop to the stunning white sand coastline. Protected areas like the Cap Ternay Marine National Park offer an opportunity to explore the incredible underwater world of the ocean and secluded island lagoons. You can also get close to the rare giant tortoises, sea turtles, or 26 species of land-dwelling crab.


The Seychelles is an idyllic tropical paradise surrounded by aquamarine crystal clear waters. Diving and snorkelling are popular activities in the beautiful resort that boasts a relaxed and laidback vibe. The capital city Victoria, on Mahé Island, is a vibrant hub and home to the National Botanical Gardens where you will find indigenous orchids, palms and other plants, as well as giant tortoises and fruit bats.
